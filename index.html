About Us

Project BlueDot is initiated with a view to digitize the travel industry. Our concept is to connect people with places in real-time and explore nature's beauty on screen. It's a live tour app. Tourism Industry is one of the worst affected areas due to COVID-19 pandemic. Millions of people lost their jobs. BlueDot project aims to minimize the effect of economic losses in the tourism areas. Our main objective is to provide a platform for local businesses like hotels, restaurants, travel agencies to promote their businesses to international and local tourists. We welcome you all to work with us. Use Our Technology for your services.
